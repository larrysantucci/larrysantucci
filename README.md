## Larry Santucci

**Senior Advisor & Research Fellow**
Federal Reserve Bank of Philadelphia · [Consumer Finance Institute](https://www.philadelphiafed.org/consumer-finance)

I study consumer credit markets, fair lending, elder financial health, housing discrimination, and AI/ML applications in economics. Co-founder of the [Center for the Restoration of Economic Data (CREED)](https://www.philadelphiafed.org/consumer-finance/creed).
